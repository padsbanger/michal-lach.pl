Styletime.

Made beauty treatments more accessible.

A beauty treatment should only be a click away. That was the idea behind Styletime

Styletime helps users book beauty treatments in a fast and simple way. Up to recently, there was no good service in Norway to do this, and many beauty salons did not even have a presence on the web.

Created while working for Schibsted Tech Polska.

Technologies used:

  • React.js
  • Redux
  • Webpack
  • LESS
  • Symfony
